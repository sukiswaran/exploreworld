Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical island nation located in the Indian Ocean. It is well known for its beautiful white-sand beaches, clear blue waters, and luxury island resorts. The country attracts tourists from around the world who come to relax and enjoy the natural beauty.

Geography

The Maldives is made up of more than 1,000 small coral islands grouped into 26 atolls. Many islands are surrounded by lagoons and coral reefs, which support a rich variety of marine life.

Culture

Maldivian culture is influenced by South Asian, Arab, and African traditions. The country has unique music, dance, and local crafts that reflect its history. Seafood is an important part of Maldivian cuisine.

Tourism

Tourism is the main industry in the Maldives. Visitors enjoy activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, island hopping, and staying in water villas at luxury resorts.

Famous Places

Popular places to visit in the Maldives include Malé City, Maafushi Island, Banana Reef, Baros Island, and Vaadhoo Island, which is famous for its glowing beach at night.
