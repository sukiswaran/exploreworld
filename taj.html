Taj Mahal Package

Visit the Symbol of Love

The Taj Mahal is one of the most beautiful monuments in the world. Located in Agra, India, this white marble masterpiece was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. This travel package allows you to experience its breathtaking architecture, history, and culture.

3 Days Taj Mahal Tour Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Agra, hotel check-in, evening local market visit.
  • Day 2: Sunrise visit to Taj Mahal, Agra Fort sightseeing, cultural tour.
  • Day 3: Mehtab Bagh garden visit and departure.
